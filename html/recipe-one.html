Classic Spaghetti Carbonara

Classic Spaghetti Carbonara is a creamy and savory Italian pasta dish that’s both comforting and delicious. Made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and pepper, this dish is a timeless favorite. It’s simple to prepare, yet its rich flavors make it feel like a special treat. Perfect for a quick weeknight dinner or a cozy weekend meal.

  • Spaghetti: 12 oz (about 340g)
  • Pancetta: 4 oz (about 115g), diced (or substitute with guanciale or bacon)
  • Eggs: 2 large
  • Parmesan Cheese: 1 cup, grated (you can also mix in some Pecorino Romano)
  • Garlic: 2 cloves, minced (optional)
  • Black Pepper: Freshly ground, to taste
  • Salt: To taste
  • Fresh Parsley: Chopped, for garnish (optional)
