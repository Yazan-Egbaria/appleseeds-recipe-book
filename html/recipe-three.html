Chocolate Lava Cake

Chocolate Lava Cake is an indulgent dessert that boasts a rich, molten chocolate center that flows out when you cut into it. This decadent treat is perfect for special occasions or when you're craving something truly luxurious. Despite its fancy presentation, it's surprisingly easy to make, and it's sure to impress anyone who takes a bite.

  • Dark Chocolate: 6 oz (about 170g), chopped
  • Unsalted Butter: 1/2 cup (1 stick), plus extra for greasing
  • Powdered Sugar: 1 cup
  • Eggs: 2 large
  • Egg Yolks: 2 large
  • All-Purpose Flour: 1/4 cup
  • Vanilla Extract: 1 teaspoon
  • Salt: A pinch
