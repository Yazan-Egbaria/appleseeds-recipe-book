Grilled Lemon Herb Chicken

This Grilled Lemon Herb Chicken is a light, healthy, and flavorful dish that’s perfect for any occasion. Marinated in a zesty lemon herb mixture, the chicken breasts are grilled to juicy perfection, offering a burst of fresh flavors in every bite. It’s an easy-to-make recipe that pairs wonderfully with a variety of sides, making it a versatile option for both casual weeknight dinners and special gatherings.

  • Chicken Breasts: 4 boneless, skinless
  • Olive Oil: 1/4 cup
  • Lemon Juice: 2 tablespoons (freshly squeezed)
  • Garlic: 3 cloves (minced)
  • Fresh Rosemary: 2 teaspoons (chopped)
  • Fresh Thyme: 2 teaspoons (chopped)
  • Salt: 1 teaspoon
  • Black Pepper: 1/2 teaspoon
